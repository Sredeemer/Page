Welcome To EPSU - UHAS.

EPSU - UHAS - Evangelical Presbyterian Student Union - is a campus based students' denomination. Our core principles are founded that of the Evangelical Presbyterian Church of Ghana. We are basically the EP Church Ghana with a touch of youthful pentecostalism. Our services are never boring, we love hymnesm, but then we do alot of contemporaries. Come worship with us at least once, and if you don't love it, you are free to go wherever you so please.

Our Mission

We exist to propagate the Gospel holistically by empowering Our members Spiritually, Materially and Socio-Culturally, to be effective agents of the Great commision.

Our Vision

Our Vision is to be dynamic agents of God, leading in great commision to bring light where there is darkness and transformating our world intp the likeness of Christ, and be self-sustaining.

Our Core Values

Our core values are just mere echoes of the core values of the Evangelical Presbyterian Church. However, EPSU UHAS has an additional core value which dictates that we are selfless vines that manifest the gifts of the Holy Spirit with love.

You just cannot be left out, We are never boring...

We have 'The DanceXetera', The Mens' Wing, The EPSU Vocals- Choir n 'Fresh oil' AND Ofcourse the "Vee Ladies Hub" for the ladies.
