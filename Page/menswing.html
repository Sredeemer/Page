We Are The Mens' Wing.

Motto: Not just heads but "Christlike" heads.

The men are the head of the family, in view of this the EPSU Mens' Wing was formed to serve as a springboard the propels the boys into men, the servants into masters, the chiefs into kings and the maestros into legends. It is important to note that this is not a group, but a fellowship sponsored by deep undying love to see young men develop in all spheres necessary for the sole purpose kingdom advance.Come join our men today as they we happily fellowship at the Lord's feet.
